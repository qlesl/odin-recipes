Cookie Recipe

Indulge in the timeless delight of Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies, perfect for any occasion! These cookies are buttery, soft, and packed with rich semisweet chocolate chips in every bite. The combination of white and brown sugar creates a chewy center with slightly crisp edges, making them irresistible. With a hint of vanilla and the option to add crunchy nuts, these cookies are easy to bake and impossible to resist. Whether you're baking for friends, family, or just a cozy treat for yourself, these cookies are sure to bring a smile to anyone's face. Serve them warm with a glass of milk for the ultimate comfort snack!

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup (150g) white sugar
  • 3/4 cup (150g) packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups (340g) semisweet chocolate chips
  • Optional: 1 cup chopped nuts (e.g., walnuts or pecans)
Instructions:
  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or lightly grease them.
  2. Cream Butter and Sugars: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Combine: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, stirring just until blended. Fold in the chocolate chips (and nuts, if using).
  5. Scoop Dough: Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  6. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown but the centers are still soft.
  7. Cool: Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.

Enjoy your warm, chewy chocolate chip cookies!
